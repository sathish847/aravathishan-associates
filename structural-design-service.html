Structural Design Services

Aaravathishan Associates offers comprehensive structural design solutions, including Structural Stability Certificates issued by a Class I A Panel Engineer for commercial, educational, healthcare, and public buildings, ensuring full compliance with NBC, IS codes, and local regulations. We also provide custom structural designs for multi-storeyed structures, delivering safe, cost-effective, and seismically resilient solutions using advanced tools like STAAD.Pro and ETABS, tailored to project specifications, site conditions, and budgetary requirements.

A Structural Stability Certificate is an official document issued by our Class I A Panel Engineer confirming the structural soundness and safety of commercial, educational, healthcare, and public buildings. It is mandatory for obtaining occupancy certificates, building renovations, license renewals, insurance approvals, and compliance with municipal corporations and fire safety regulations.

We provide safe, economical, and fully code-compliant structural designs for multi-storeyed residential, commercial, and institutional projects using advanced software like STAAD.Pro and ETABS. Our designs incorporate seismic analysis, wind load calculations, optimized reinforcement detailing, and material efficiency, tailored to site conditions, client budget, and adherence to NBC, IS 456, IS 1893, and local bylaws.
