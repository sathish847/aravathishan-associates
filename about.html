Who We Are

Inspiring Values for Banking & Finance

Aaravathishan Associates is a leading valuation and engineering consultancy firm specializing in Land and Building Valuation across India, committed to delivering accurate, transparent, and compliant property valuation services to Banks, Financial Institutions, and Government Organizations. The firm plays a pivotal role in supporting India’s banking and financial ecosystem by providing credible, defensible, and insight-driven valuation reports essential for loan approvals, mortgage security, asset monitoring, and recovery assessments. Empanelled with several Public Sector Banks, Private Banks, and NBFCs, Aaravathishan Associates adheres strictly to Indian Valuation Standards (IVS) and all regulatory frameworks prescribed by the Reserve Bank of India (RBI), the Companies Act, and professional bodies such as the Indian Institution of Valuers (IIV), Institution of Valuers (IOV), and the Council of Architecture (COA), wherever applicable. Its unwavering commitment to professional integrity, technical excellence, and regulatory compliance ensures that every valuation upholds the highest standards of accuracy, transparency, and reliability.

Our Role in the Banking Ecosystem

Supporting India's Financial Sector

At Aaravathishan Associates, we serve as a trusted pillar in India's banking and financial sector by delivering credible, defensible, and insight-driven valuation reports that underpin critical lending decisions. Our valuations are not just numbers—they are strategic tools that enable banks, NBFCs, and financial institutions to manage risk, ensure regulatory compliance, and facilitate seamless financial operations.

Loan Approvals & Risk Mitigation

RBI-compliant valuations deliver precise LTV ratios, fair market value, and realizable value—enabling confident, risk-minimized lending decisions.

Mortgage Security & Compliance

Detailed collateral verification ensures legal soundness, physical integrity, and encumbrance-free status, fully aligned with RBI & IBA guidelines.

Asset Monitoring & Recovery

Periodic revaluations and distress-sale analysis empower NPA management and SARFAESI proceedings with actionable, defensible insights.

Regulatory Adherence & Professional Standards

Ensuring Compliance and Excellence

At Aaravathishan Associates, every valuation and engineering deliverable is grounded in uncompromising regulatory adherence and professional standards. We strictly follow Indian Valuation Standards (IVS 2018) for methodology, transparency, and reporting consistency, while our empanelment with public sector banks, private banks, and NBFCs ensures full alignment with RBI Master Directions on asset classification, LTV norms, and SARFAESI compliance. Led by a Registered Valuer recognized by the Indian Institution of Valuers (IIV) and Institution of Valuers (IOV), our practice upholds ethical rigor and technical excellence. Additionally, structural designs and stability certifications are issued by COA-compliant Class I A Panel Engineers, guaranteeing code-compliant and legally sound outputs. Through a rigorous multi-level review process, we deliver audit-ready, defensible reports that consistently meet the scrutiny of regulators, financial institutions, and legal authorities.
